Frequently Asked Questions


How do I delete my check-in times?

You can delete hashtag sets by swiping left on each log in the home screen, or tapping on the delete button.


How do I edit existing logs?

Currently, you cannot edit existing logs.


What types of work check-ins does the app support?

If your workplace supports "time-ins" or "check-ins" by scanning a QR code to sign in, then you can use this app to directly scan the code, open the website, and sign in.
Once finished, press "Done", and the app will log your check-in by date and time.
