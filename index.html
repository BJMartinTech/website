Apps for Gamers

We make apps to empower your gaming experience even when you're not gaming.

Our goal at Pixelated Controller is to create everyday apps that helps enrich your life as a gamer. We aim to provide tools, resources, and all kinds of power-ups to make things like managing your backlog easier.
We have many ideas in the pipeline, and recently launched our first app, Cartridge, to help keep your backlog slim and manageable. Download it now and give it a try!
In the future, we're looking at apps for managing your collection, tracking video game puzzles, and more. To stay updated on future updates, follow us on Twitter and bookmark our blog.
